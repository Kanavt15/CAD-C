About LungAI

Advancing Medical Imaging with Deep Learning Technology

Our Mission

We're committed to revolutionizing lung cancer detection through the power of artificial intelligence and deep learning. Our advanced 3D CNN technology aims to provide healthcare professionals with accurate, reliable, and efficient tools for early cancer detection.

By combining cutting-edge computer vision, medical imaging expertise, and state-of-the-art neural networks, we're helping save lives through earlier and more accurate diagnosis.

The Technology Behind LungAI

Three advanced AI models working together for comprehensive lung cancer detection

Improved 3D CNN (Residual + SE)

Model 1

Our foundational 3D Convolutional Neural Network with Residual connections and Squeeze-Excitation blocks. Analyzes entire volumetric data for comprehensive spatial understanding of lung structures.

  • Accuracy: 83.3% | F1: 0.67
  • Residual connections + SE attention
  • Reliable baseline performance

EfficientNet3D-B2

Model 2

State-of-the-art EfficientNet-B2 adapted for 3D medical imaging with compound scaling. Features MBConv blocks with Squeeze-Excitation and optimized depth/width for superior feature learning.

  • Accuracy: 93.1% | F1: 0.73
  • MBConv + Compound Scaling
  • 9.2M parameters for complex patterns

DenseNet3D + Attention

Best Model

Our most accurate model combining DenseNet's feature reuse with multi-head spatial attention. Dense blocks with 4-head attention mechanism capture intricate patterns with exceptional efficiency.

  • Accuracy: 95.7% | F1: 0.78
  • Dense Blocks + 4-Head Attention
  • Only 673K params (most efficient)

Ensemble Intelligence

System

All three models analyze each image simultaneously, providing multiple expert opinions. This multi-model approach increases reliability and provides comprehensive diagnostic insights.

  • 3 independent AI assessments
  • Cross-validation of predictions
  • Enhanced diagnostic confidence
